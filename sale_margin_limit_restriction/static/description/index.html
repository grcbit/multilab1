Minimum Sales Margin Restriction for Sales Order

Minimum Sales Margin on Product and Product Category

Sales Margin Minimum Percentage Policy on Product and Category Odoo App

This app allows your sales manager to set the minimum margin on product variant (product) and product category form and compare it with the computed sales margin percentage (Odoo Standard) on the sale order line.

Main Features:

  • Allow your sales manager to configure the minimum margin limit on product and product categories. Please note that the first preference will be product category and if there is no configuration found then product min sales margin will be applied.
  • Restrict the sale order for confirming if the product margin is greater than the sales order line computed sales margin.
  • This feature is of restriction on confirmation of sales order will not be applicable to a website sales order / online shop orders.
  • For more details please check the video and check the below screenshots.
